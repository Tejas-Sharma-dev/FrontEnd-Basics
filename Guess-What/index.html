Guess What!!

Get ready to test your emoji knowledge! This game challenges you to decipher emojis and guess the corresponding words or phrases. Here's how to play:

  1. Start the game: Click the "Start Game" button to begin your emoji guessing adventure.

  2. Decipher the emojis: Each question will be displayed using one or more emojis. These emojis hint at a person, place, thing, or movie.

  3. Type your guess: Type your guess and press Enter to submit your answer.

  4. See if you're right: The game will instantly reveal whether your guess was correct or incorrect.

  5. Time is ticking: you have 2 minutes to guess as many questions as possible. So, think fast and be accurate!

  6. Keep guessing: New emoji questions will continue to appear as you answer them. Keep guessing until the timer runs out.

  7. Scoreboard: Once the time is up, your score will be displayed. The higher your score, the better your emoji decoding skills!

Ready to put your emoji expertise to the test? Click "Start Game" and let's see how many emojis you can crack!
